What is the consumer surplus calculator?
The consumer surplus calculator is a tool that determines the difference between what consumers are willing to pay for a product versus its market price.
How to calculate consumer surplus?
To calculate consumer surplus you should use the following formula:
consumer surplus = willing price - actual price;
Why use consumer surplus calculator?
The consumer surplus calculator is used in order to measure the difference between what consumers are willing to pay for a product versus its market price.
